The case of AMT

 

3 types of participants

Vendor, requester, worker

There are three types of participants: A vendor (in this case, it is Amazon), a requester (person, company, organization, etc.) who publish a task and a worker (or Turker) who accomplish those tasks. In this aspect Amazon provides a platform similar to a job board where requesters release HITs (jobs) and demand workers to complete them. A requester will only pay workers when they deem the work good enough. This way, firms can access a very large workforce whenever they want and get thousands of HITs finished in a small amount of time. Moreover, it allows them to pay only when they are satisfied with the results, insuring a good quality control.
Traditional labor processes are generally extremely expensive in terms of money or internal resources and can lead to a waste of time, effectiveness and therefore efficiency. The rise of crowdsourcing and the constant evolution of new technologies could reflect a promising future for a successful business development as it allows addressing those issues.

It is difficult to give a precise number of AMT users because Amazon keeps that information a secret. However, a 2015 World Bank report estimated around 500,000 registered workers worldwide, although not all of them were active. According to Alexa.com – a web tracking company also owned by Amazon – Mechanical Turk had about 750,000 unique visitors worldwide in December 2015. There was an average of 1,278 active requesters per day in 2015 according to data collected by Professor Ipeirotis’ mturk-tracker tool [6]. By november 2017, there was up to 35 000 HITs on the website waiting to be completed by workers. Requester can publish 250,000 tasks maximum in a batch.
The variety of HITs available on the website is very wide and includes consumer products, finance, ethics, education, etc. Requester can manage HITS they've published, decide on the deadline and specify the qualification required to accomplish such a task.
Usually, the remuneration of Turkers is very cheap because requesters don’t demand any specialized skills. The amount of money earned depends on the number of tasks done. In general, a HITs is paid between 0.35 centimes and 1 euros. Workers usually use AMT for extra pocket money rather than salary.
